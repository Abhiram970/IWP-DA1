Nutrients in Our Food

Nutrients are the substances in food that our bodies process to enable it to function. Your nutrient requirements are influenced by factors including your age, growth stage and activity. Nutrients are so small that the naked eye can't see them.

There are 6 essential nutrients that the body needs to function properly. Nutrients are compounds in foods essential to life and health, providing us with energy, the building blocks for repair and growth and substances necessary to regulate chemical processes.


  There are five major nutrients and they are :

  • Carbohydrates
  • Proteins
  • Fats
  • Vitamins
  • Minerals
